Points of interest
in Odaiba

Attractive shopping complexes and amusement parks near our hotel*Free Shuttle Bus Available

Directions on Google Maps

DiverCity Tokyo Plaza

Shopping

This large shopping complex is located in the water front, offering entertainments and a wide variety of dining options. A great way to spend a day for shopping international and local brands.

AQUA CiTY ODAIBA

Shopping

Located right next to Odaiba Marine Park, this large shopping complex offers a great view of central Tokyo with overlooking Statue of Liberty and Rainbow Bridge. You can find more than 80 stores including international and local casual clothing brands as well as a wide variety of dining options.

DECKS Tokyo Beach

Shopping

This large shopping complex offers diverse entertainment options such as indoor amusement park "Joypolis", kids' favourite "LEGOLAND", "Madame Tussauds Tokyo" with celebrities' wax figures and "Takoyaki Museum".

VenusFort

Shopping

In this shopping complex, you can find an outlet floor with international and local brands including LAOX. Get immersed in a medieval European town with lights replicating the sunset and sunrise.

Ooedo-Onsen Monogatari

Onsen

This Onsen theme park offers 13 different types of baths including popular open-air bath. Dress up in yukata, a casual summer kimono, and enjoy authentic Japanese festive ambiance.

EPSON teamLab Borderless

Attractions

Internationally known digital art team, teamLab offers the world's first digital art museum. Immerse yourself with creativity, colour and endless imagination. Perfect for your Instagram shots.

MORE

Toyosu Market

Food

Relocated from Tsukiji market, this modern wholesale market opened in October, 2018. It offers tourists with an opportunity to view market actions from observation windows. Experience their Sushi, seafood rice bowl and famous Japanese omelette.

Tokyo Big Sight (Tokyo International Exhibition Center)

Event

One of Japan's largest convention centers host a variety of events such as Tokyo Motor Show and Comic Market.

National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation

Attractions

Japan's premier science museum located in Odaiba. Experience the future, and see how the latest Japanese technologies will impact our life in the future.

Giant Sky Wheel in palette town

Attractions

This Giant Ferris wheel is an iconic attraction of Odaiba. The illuminations at night reflect seasonal looks. Enjoy spectacular views of Tokyo Tower and Tokyo Skytree.

MEGA WEB

Attractions

TOYOTA's automobile theme park titled "look, ride and feel" attracts all visitors. Test drive your favourite cars and feel their historic cars. Children get to know traffic rules and have fun driving experience.

Water Bus

Attractions

Tokyo Cruise operates water buses between Odaiba Marine Park and Asakusa by passing under Rainbow Bridge. Take advantages of their transportation services allowing you to enjoy a wonderful view of Tokyo.
